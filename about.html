About Us

Way back in 1963, a man named Parry started his small entrepreneurial business on the Caringbah High street in the hopes of bringing a little bit of colour and playfulness to the area. almost sixty years on, and under new ownership, we at Parry’s Milkbar and Candystore aim to preserve that colour and playfulness, as well as the 60’s Diner aesthetic which has remained since the Good Ol'e Days.
